Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support PNG files only. We're working on adding support for more formats.

Is there a file size limit?

For optimal performance, we recommend files under 10MB, but larger files may work depending on your device.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.

Can I change multiple colors at once?

Currently, you can change one color at a time. Apply changes and repeat the process for multiple colors.
